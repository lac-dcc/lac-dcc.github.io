Compilers Lab's Tool Box

The Compilers Lab is part of the Department of Computer Science of the Federal University of Minas Gerais, Brazil. We are located in the city of Belo Horizonte, the capital of the state of Minas Gerais. The Compilers Lab was created in August 24th, 2018. The Compilers Research Group was created in Dezember 2nd, 2014.
